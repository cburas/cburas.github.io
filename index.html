Classic Titles - Modern Titles - What do you Think?

Today it seems as if every movie isn't based off a screenplay but a book. When we were children, the trend was the same but we were far less aware of it. That's why it's so surprising when we learn a story we have only categorized as a movie turns out to be based on a novel.

This site names and describes those novels, some well known, some not. The titles are categorized into two sections: Classic and Modern. In this instance, classic titles are considered anything published before 1922 and modern titles are anything since then. The date 1922 was chosen as this is the current cut-off date for works in the public domain until January 1, 2019.
